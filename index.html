Choral reefs around the world are vanishing at an unprecedented rate. A team of divers, photographers and scientists set out on a thrilling ocean adventure to discover why and to reveal the underwater mystery to the world.

The Impact of

Healthy Choral at Normal Temperature

At an unchanged temperature chorals live happily. The ocean ecosystem and food supply to 50% of the worlds population are intact.

Choral Bleaching - A Deadly Fever

When the temperature increases with 2 celcius degrees, the chorals catches a deadly fever and turn bleach. If the temperature does not decrease,the health of the chorals are in danger.

From Fever to Death

If the temperature stays too hot for too long or increases the chorals get overcooked and die. The ecosystem breaks down. The ocean life are in danger. This has happened in Hawaii, Great Barrier Reef and Bahamas and many more places.
